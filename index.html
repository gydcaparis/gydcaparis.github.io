About Us

The Global Youth Dialogue among Civilizations Alliance (GYDCA) was founded in response to the increasing need for intercultural dialogue in an era of globalization and digitalization. As interactions between different cultures and civilizations have expanded, so have misunderstandings and conflicts, particularly among youth. GYDCA was created to provide a platform for young people worldwide to engage in meaningful cultural exchange, promoting mutual understanding and respect.

The establishment of GYDCA is closely linked to China’s “Global Civilization Dialogue” initiative. In 2013, Chinese President Xi Jinping proposed the “Civilization Dialogue” initiative, advocating for dialogue to enhance mutual understanding and trust among civilizations, promoting the diversity of world cultures and their common development. The further development of this initiative in 2023 inspired the founding of GYDCA, offering a broader space for youth engagement and participation in global dialogue.

Moreover, GYDCA has received strong support from UNESCO. UNESCO has long been committed to promoting cultural diversity and peace worldwide. In the context of rapid technological advancements and globalization, intercultural dialogue has become increasingly vital. Through its collaboration with UNESCO and other international organizations, GYDCA aims to foster the protection of world cultural heritage and engage young people in addressing global challenges through dialogue and innovative solutions.
