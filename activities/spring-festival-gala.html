Prelude to the Spring Festival Gala

Date: January 2025

This event was a vibrant prelude to the upcoming Spring Festival, featuring a variety of cultural performances, interactive workshops, and traditional activities. It offered a taste of the festive spirit of the most important holiday in Chinese culture and served as a bridge for cultural understanding and exchange among global youth.
