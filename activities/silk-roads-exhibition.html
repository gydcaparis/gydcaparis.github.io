Architecture, Monuments, and Urban Planning along the Silk Roads

Date: November 2024

This exhibition explored the rich history of the Silk Roads through its architecture, monuments, and urban planning. It was an immersive journey that revealed how cultural exchange shaped the landscapes and cities of this ancient trade route. The event highlighted the shared heritage of the diverse civilizations that interacted along the Silk Roads, promoting cross-cultural appreciation.
