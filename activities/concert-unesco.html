Concert at UNESCO by the CNTO Chinese National Traditional Orchestra

Date: October 2024

This unique concert, held at UNESCO headquarters, showcased the rich traditions of Chinese music. The performance by the CNTO Chinese National Traditional Orchestra was a powerful example of cultural dialogue through art, bringing together diverse audiences to appreciate a shared human heritage. It highlighted the importance of preserving and promoting traditional music in a global context.
