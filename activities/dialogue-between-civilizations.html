International Forum on Dialogue Between Civilizations and the Youth of the World

Date : June 10, 2025

Location: UNESCO Headquarters, 7 Place de Fontenoy, 75007 Paris

Description:

This conference aims to bring together young people from around the world who have led innovative actions, strengthening exchanges in areas such as education, culture, and innovation. The goal is to foster communication and understanding between youth, while contributing to the UNESCO-supported mission of dialogue between civilizations. Numerous speakers, including young leaders, experts, and actors from education, art, innovation, and other sectors, will participate. The event will focus on intercultural cooperation, highlighting the talents of global youth within a diverse international context.

Results:

  • Number of participants : 200
  • Facilitation of international dialogue and new cooperation opportunities
  • Promotion of innovative projects (awards presented)
  • Support for UNESCO’s mission
  • Participation of international artists to highlight intangible cultures
